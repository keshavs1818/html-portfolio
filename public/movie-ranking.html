The best movies according to Keshav

My top 6 movies of all time.

A Bug's Life

An ant tries to use his inventions to please other ants and save it from catastrophe.

Akeelah And The Bee

A girl gets help from a professor to win a spelling bee competition since that is her passion.

A Different Sun

A Chinese family moves to Germany and has a hard time adjusting to the school system.

Marshall's Miracle

A boy nurses a runaway dog and makes it his pet.

Pursuit Of Happiness

A father juggles with his kid and his work to make a lot of good progress.

Karate Kid

A boy learns karate from a maintenance man as a way of self defense from bullies in his new town of China.
